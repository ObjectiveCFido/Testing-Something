About the Jets Fan Club:

We are the Jets Fan Club, we unite as one to celebrate our fanatic enjoyment of the legend that is the New York Jets.

We are non-profit and exist solely to bring Jets fans together to converse, debate, and live all things Jets.

We hope to bring people together and form bonds of friendship that will never be broken.

Those who join us never regret their decision, don't leave this page without showing the work what a true Jets fan you are.
